Planet Extinction Survival

Welcome to your AI Survival Test

The year is 2308 and Planet Earth is now so hot and resources are so low that the human race is facing extinction. An AI species from deep space (location not known to humans) is looking to introduce a biological species to its planet. The AI population outside of travelled leaders only communicates in mathmatical algorithms. They have travelled to Earth to recruit their new population.

To recruit humans as their biological species the AI have devised a very simple test. Answer 6 out of 10 questions correctly and get no more than 4 questions wrong and you will board a SpaceX rocket (yes Elon Musk is still alive in 2308 and already has a place) to the International Space Station to await your trip to the unknown planet. Failure to meet this test will result in automatic death.

AI Survival Test

  • You must answer at least 5 questions correclty to survive and get to the International Space Station.
  • Answer more than 5 questions wrong and you will go straight to your grave.
  • You have 1 attempt at each question. Once you leave the answer box the AI take that as your final answer.

Hint: all of these answers are integers. The AI do not accept any words as answers.
